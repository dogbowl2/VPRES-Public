Privacy Policy

NES Collection Manager · Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the NES Collection Manager mobile application (the “App”) handles your information. Your privacy is important, and the App is built to keep your data entirely on your own device.

Summary

The App does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any personal data. It works fully offline. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Information We Collect

None. The App does not collect any personal or sensitive user data. It does not ask you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

How Your Data Is Stored

Any information you create in the App — such as your game collection, component details, and notes — is stored locally on your device in a private application database. This data is never uploaded to us or to any third party. If you uninstall the App, this locally stored data is removed by the operating system.

Network Access

The App is designed to operate offline. It does not require an internet connection to function and does not send your collection data over the network.

Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Permissions

The App requests only the permissions required for its core, on-device features. It does not access your contacts, location, microphone, or camera for the purpose of collecting personal data.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It contains no advertising and no data-collection mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
