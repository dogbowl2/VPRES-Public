Privacy Policy

CartLodge · Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the CartLodge mobile application (the “App”) handles your information. The App is designed to keep your personal data on your own device.

Summary

The App does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any personal data, and contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking. There are no accounts and no sign-in. Your collection data is stored only on your device. The App uses the camera for on-device barcode scanning, and uses an internet connection only to download game cover images and to open optional external reference links in your browser, as described below. An optional “Float Mode” feature, which you start yourself, can capture your screen to identify a game; those frames are analyzed on your device and are never stored or transmitted.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or sensitive user data. The App does not ask you to create an account, sign in, or provide personal information, and it has no analytics, advertising, or tracking components.

Your Collection Data (Stored On-Device)

Information you create in the App — such as your game collection, component details, linked barcodes, and notes — is stored locally on your device in a private application database. This data is never uploaded to us. If you uninstall the App, this locally stored data is removed by the operating system. You may export a backup of your own data using your device’s share feature; if you do, you choose where that backup file goes.

Camera

The App can use your device’s camera only to scan a game barcode when you open the scanner. The barcode is read on your device and matched against the App’s local game database. Camera images are not saved, stored, or transmitted anywhere. If you choose to link a scanned barcode to a game, only the barcode number is saved locally in your collection database.

Screen Capture (Float Mode)

The App includes an optional feature called Float Mode, which identifies which game is shown on your screen (for example, in a marketplace listing or video) so you can find it in your collection. Float Mode runs only when you start it yourself and grant Android’s screen-capture consent. While it is running, it uses the system screen-capture (MediaProjection) service to read frames of your screen, which are analyzed entirely on your device — matching the on-screen image and text against the App’s local game catalog.

Captured screen frames are processed in memory and immediately discarded. No screen content is recorded, saved, or transmitted off your device, and none of it is sent to us or any third party. Only the resulting match — which game in your catalog was recognized — is shown to you in the App. While Float Mode is active, an ongoing notification and a floating on-screen indicator are always visible so you know capture is in progress. You can pause or stop it at any time; Android’s screen-capture consent is single-use, so capture also ends when you lock your screen and must be explicitly granted again to resume.

Internet Access

The App uses an internet connection for two limited purposes:

  • Game cover images: some cover artwork is downloaded from third-party image hosts so it can be displayed in the App. Making such a request necessarily reveals your device’s IP address to the host serving the image, as with loading any web image. We do not send any of your collection data or personal information with these requests.
  • Optional external links: the App can open a price-reference page (PriceCharting) for a game in your device’s external web browser when you tap to do so. Once opened, that website is governed by its own privacy policy. The App does not send your personal or collection data to that site.

The App does not transmit your collection or personal data over the network.

Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party network interactions are the image hosts that serve cover artwork and, if you tap an external link, the reference website opened in your browser.

Permissions

  • Camera — used only for on-device barcode scanning, as described above.
  • Internet — used only to download cover images and open optional external links.
  • Screen capture (MediaProjection, via a foreground service) — used only while you run the optional Float Mode feature, to read screen frames that are analyzed on-device and immediately discarded; never stored or transmitted.
  • Display over other apps — used only to show the Float Mode floating bubble (recognition result and controls) over your screen; it reads no data.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children, and contains no advertising.
